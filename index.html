About Me

Ful Clinton Micah was born on 24 April 1997 in Bafia Muyuka, Southwest Region of Cameroon. He started his career by writing songs and sending them to his friends for feedback. He received so many positive reviews which encouraged him to step into the music scene.

Micah Light wrote his first song titled “Why not give him all the praise” in the year 2017 and in 2020, the song was reallised with some other 8 tracks making it already an album in his small home studio known as "Light Record". His stage name is MICAH LIGHT and he's a Gospel song writer, singer and a keyboardlist
